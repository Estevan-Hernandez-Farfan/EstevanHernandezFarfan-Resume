Estevan Hernandez Farfan

Musician

I have decided to pursue my dreams of becoming a professional musician. To some this might seem as a daunting task. I once was under the impression that I should get a normal job and make normal money to live a normal life. While that is all fine and good in its own right, I felt there was something more that I wanted to pursue.

Over the past couple of months, I have been doubting myself about what my current path is to becoming an English major. I just felt that it truly didn’t fit the criteria that I was looking for when it came to a fulfilling career. I had dabbled in many art forms throughout my life, but I was always told that they should be kept as hobbies

I never started looking at the possibility that it could be something much more than that. I am now currently a Music major looking to pursue a career performing at various venues/jobs.
